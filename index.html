About Me

Ivan is a spatial data analyst and a GIS expert who has experience in disaster management activities and delivering training to empower the community. He has a great passion in open source software. He is a curious person and love to try and explore new things related to computer and technologies. He likes to see problems as challenge to improve himself.

Experience

UGM Center for the Development of Spatial Data Infrastructure

Research Assistant

UGM Center for the Development of Spatial Data Infrastructure (Pusat Pengembangan Infrastruktur Data Spasial FT UGM) is a research center in UGM focusing on the development of spatial data infrastructure. His role was giving assistance to the research conducted by the researcher. Some of the research activities were acquiring field position data, developing and delivering training materials.
